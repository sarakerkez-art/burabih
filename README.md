## 💡 What is Bura?

Bura translates air quality data into simple, daily guidance.

Instead of showing numbers, it tells families what to do — in their language, every day.

Example:

“Tonight: don’t reload the stove after 9 PM.  
Ventilate at 2 PM — that’s the cleanest air today.  
Keep children indoors until 11 AM tomorrow.”

## ⚡ Key Features

- **Daily Guidance First**  
  Clear, actionable recommendations instead of raw data

- **Buri (Voice Layer)**  
  A lightweight animated wind element that communicates one simple sentence

- **Light Personalisation**  
  Guidance adapts to household context

- **Alert-Ready**  
  Designed for daily delivery via email or messaging

  ## 🌍 Why It Matters

Air quality data exists — but it rarely leads to action.

Bura acts as a citizen-facing layer that turns environmental data into everyday decisions.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- AI: Claude API
- Data: aqicn.org / Federal HMZ BiH
